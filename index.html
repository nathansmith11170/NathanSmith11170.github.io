About Me

Hi there. I'm Jeff Morhous, a 2nd year student in Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University.

I love technology and creating new things, which is why I’m pursuing a career in Software Engineering. Last Summer, I had the opportunity to intern at Fidelity investments, where I loved putting the fundamentals I learned in my first year of school to practical use on a project that made the workflow and releases easier. I’m a very hands on learner, so I like to work on side projects, and have recently been pretty engrossed in learning the Android ecosystem.

Outside of school and work, I like to play guitar, hike, work on cars, and act as a servant leader in my church. I’m always looking for the best opportunity to expand my knowledge and skill set while making a positive impact.
